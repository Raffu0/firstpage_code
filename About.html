Who is Cristiano Ronaldo?

Main page Teams

As a young child Ronaldo used to skip classes and school to play football, he was obsessed. He was only 8 years old when his football "career" started as Ronaldo began playing for a team called Andorinha, where his dad helped out. He later moved to another club called Nacional, where after just one season he moved to Sporting CP.

Ronaldo is a proud father of 5, his first son being Cristiano Jr., who was born in 2010. He then got twins named Eva and Mateo via a surrogate mother in 2017.
Later he had a daughter, Alana Martina. In 2022, they sadly lost a twin in childbirth.
He often posts pictures with his children and family and says that being a father is one of his greatest joys of life. Ronaldo tries to give his children a normal life and teaches them respect and discipline.

Ronaldo is widely known for his dedication. Teammates have said that he trains harder than anyone else - first to arrive, last to leave.
Even during holidays, vacations and off days he sticks to his strict training and diet.

Ronaldo doesn't drink or smoke, he eats clean, sleeps well and takes his health very serious. Many people think that Ronaldo is arrogant but many close friends to Ronaldo say that he's extremely confident and sets high standards for himself.

Ronaldo has over 600 million followers on Instagram, which makes him the most followed person in the world! Ronaldo speaks Portuguese, Spanish and English. He doesn't use his fame for endorements only but also to share family moments, traning routines and postivie messages!

Ronaldo's birthplace: Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.
